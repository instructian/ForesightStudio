# Outline of the 2×2 Foresight Method

## Purpose

The 2×2 method is a structured scenario planning tool that explores
uncertainty by crossing two critical uncertainties to create four
plausible futures.

## Steps

1.  **Frame the Focal Question**
    -   Define the strategic decision.
    -   Establish the time horizon.
2.  **Gather and Cluster Inputs**
    -   Collect signals, trends, and drivers.
    -   Cluster related signals into broader themes.
3.  **Identify Critical Uncertainties**
    -   Select drivers with high impact and high uncertainty.
4.  **Select Two Critical Uncertainties and Define Axes**
    -   Choose the two most important uncertainties.
    -   Define opposite endpoints and build the 2×2 matrix.
5.  **Develop Four Scenarios**
    -   Name each scenario.
    -   Write a narrative.
    -   Identify assumptions, implications, and early indicators.
6.  **Test Strategies and Reflect**
    -   Evaluate strategies across all scenarios.
    -   Identify no-regret moves, contingent strategies, and strategic
        risks.

## Why the Method Works

-   Encourages thinking beyond prediction.
-   Produces four plausible futures.
-   Supports resilient strategic planning.
