## Six Archetypes
1. Growth & Decay
2. Threats & New Hopes
3. Wasteworlds
4. The Powers that Be
5. Disarray
6. Inversion

These are thinking tools derived by Fergnani & Song (2020) from analysis of 140 science fiction films.

## Applying Archetypes
Growth & Decay → corporate migration systems.
Threats & New Hopes → global climate visas.
Wasteworlds → post-collapse migration.
The Powers that Be → algorithmic border control.
Disarray → fractured institutions.
Inversion → AI/ecological governance.

## Building Scenarios
1. Identify signals.
2. Cluster into themes.
3. Apply an archetype.
4. Build a world.
5. Test assumptions.
6. Compare worlds.

## Core Principle
Signals become themes.
Themes become scenarios through archetypes.
Scenarios improve strategic preparedness.
